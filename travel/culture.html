Guelaguetza

The Guelaguetza is one of the most celebrated cultural gatherings of whole Mexico. It is celebrated in July from the 23rd-30th of this year.

People from all of the local communitiess gather up to celebrate their traditional costums and dances.

Aside from traditional dances there are many cultural events such as theather, live music and many more activities for the family.
